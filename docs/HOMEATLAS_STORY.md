# The HomeAtlas Story

**Why this company exists — and why the software exists.**

---

## Before the Platform

SqueegeeKing started the way many great service companies start: one bucket, one squeegee, one customer who trusted us to care about their home.

Noah Thomas and Dasan Gramps built a window cleaning and home care business in Chico, California on a simple belief — **every home deserves consistent care, and trust is earned through craftsmanship.**

Years of work produced something rare in home services: real relationships, repeat customers, and a reputation that compounds. Revenue happened. Referrals happened. But the **story of each home** lived in memory, texts, and scattered photos — not in a system worthy of the care itself.

---

## The Frustration That Created HomeAtlas

Generic CRMs treat a home like a job ticket. Scheduling apps treat technicians like dots on a map. Websites template "ABC Window Cleaning" and call it a day.

None of them answer the question a premium homeowner actually asks:

> *"What has been done for my home — and what should we do next?"*

Founders Noah and Dasan needed something different:
- A way to **remember where they came from** (legacy revenue, milestones, founder story)
- A way to **see the company as it is today** (live revenue, reviews, membership)
- A way to **present care** that feels bespoke — as if the Home Care Plan were written for one property alone

HomeAtlas is the answer to that need — first for SqueegeeKing, eventually for other companies who hold the same standard.

---

## What We Believe

From `lib/admin/company-philosophy.ts` — these are not marketing lines. They are the company constitution:

1. **Every home deserves consistent care.**
2. **We build trust through craftsmanship.**
3. **We create systems that outlast us.**
4. **Every customer becomes part of the SqueegeeKing family.**

Headquarters exists because of the third belief. The Home Care Plan exists because of the first and fourth. HomeAtlas exists because the third requires software that survives the founders' daily involvement — software that documents the life of a property for decades.

---

## Two Timelines

SqueegeeKing carries **two timelines** simultaneously:

### Legacy

Everything before the operating system — founded date, early milestones, lifetime revenue and homes protected before HomeAtlas tracked jobs formally. This history is **honored, not hidden**. It appears in Headquarters, Our Story, and the Legacy Honor card.

### Operating System

Everything from OS launch forward — closed jobs, ARR, plans created, members, live Google Reviews. This is the **living company** — numbers that change, charts that draw, missions that update.

A company is not only its last quarter. HomeAtlas holds both truths without flattening them into one misleading chart.

---

## The Home Care Plan Moment

The flagship customer experience is the **Home Care Plan** — not a PDF proposal, not a quote email, a **digital experience**:

- Property-specific findings and photography
- Honest recommendations
- Membership comparison with clarity, not pressure
- A path to become part of the family

When a homeowner scrolls through their plan, they should feel the same calm precision as opening a luxury product page. That feeling is the standard every HomeAtlas tenant must meet.

---

## Headquarters

Internal product copy defines Headquarters simply:

> *We built Headquarters to remember where we came from, understand where we are, and make wise decisions about where we're going.*

When Noah unlocks `/hq` in the evening, the interface greets him by name, breathes into focus, and surfaces what Atlas noticed that morning. That is not vanity — it is **founder alignment**. Decisions made elsewhere drift; decisions made in Headquarters reference truth.

---

## SqueegeeKing → HomeAtlas

**SqueegeeKing** remains what homeowners hire. **HomeAtlas** is what operators run.

The relationship is intentional:
- Homeowners never need to learn the word "HomeAtlas" to receive excellent care.
- Operators eventually will — when the platform opens to partner companies who want SqueegeeKing-grade presentation without building from scratch.

SqueegeeKing is tenant zero — the proof that the OS works in the real world, with real jobs, real reviews, and real founders checking the numbers every day.

---

## The Long Arc

We are not building software to sell software. We are building the infrastructure for **documenting how great home care companies care for homes** — beautifully, permanently, and with respect for both the homeowner and the people doing the work.

If we succeed, a homeowner in 2036 opens their portal and scrolls ten years of visits, photos, and recommendations like a cherished album. The founder who runs that company opens Headquarters and sees the same story from the inside — revenue, reputation, and mission aligned.

That is why HomeAtlas exists.
