# Atlas Pricing Engine — Founding Specification

**HomeAtlas Internal · July 4, 2026**

---

## North Star

Every price generated by HomeAtlas should be:

- **Technically consistent**
- **Emotionally understandable**
- **Easy for a team member to explain to a homeowner**

The engine exists to create **trust first** and **consistency second** — not just to perform calculations.

## Company Law

All pricing math flows through:

```
lib/pricing/company-settings.ts   ← rates, multipliers, limits (no magic numbers)
lib/pricing/window-care-pricing.ts ← formulas (Atlas Pricing Engine v1.0)
```

Future HQ panel: **⚙ Pricing Settings** — edit `COMPANY_SETTINGS` without deployment.
